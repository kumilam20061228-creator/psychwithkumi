# Reflections on my first ever BPS student committee meeting
![2026-02-20-reflections-bps-chair-elect](../images/international.jpg)

On the 20th of February, 2026, I had the opportunity to visit the head office of The British Psychological Society (BPS) and attend the Student Committee meeting in person - it was utterly inspiring to hear all the impressive proposals.


Being in a room filled with all the talented, passionate students who will shape the future generation of Psychology, the atmosphere is charged with enthusiasm as we exchange ideas and share insights together. I was grateful for the opportunity to present my proposal as a Chair-Elect of the Student Committee, focusing on strengthening engagement with international students. 


As an international student myself, I recognised the challenges students face, especially when they are transitioning to a new academic environment. My position as a Chair-Elect then offered me the ability to flag and reflect on these barriers. 


Thank you to the members who attended the meeting. I am excited to see what we can achieve together in the year ahead!
